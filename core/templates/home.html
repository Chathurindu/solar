Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our solar products and services? Check out our FAQ section for answers to common questions about solar panel installations, financing options, maintenance, and more. If you don't see your question answered, feel free to reach out to us for more information.

  • How does solar energy work?

    Solar panels use photovoltaic (PV) cells to convert sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity. An inverter then converts the DC electricity into alternating current (AC) electricity that can be used to power homes and businesses.

  • What are the benefits of using solar energy?

    Solar energy is a renewable and clean energy source that can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions, lower energy bills, increase energy independence, and boost property values.

  • How much do solar panels cost?

    The cost of solar panels can vary widely depending on the size and capacity of the system, the quality of the components, and the complexity of the installation process. It's best to contact a reputable solar products and services provider to get a customized quote based on your specific energy needs and budget.

  • Can solar panels work during a power outage?

    Solar panels alone cannot power a home during a power outage, but a solar panel system with battery backup can provide power during an outage.

  • Do I need to replace my roof before installing solar panels?

    It's recommended to have a roof in good condition before installing solar panels. If your roof is older or in need of repairs, it may be best to replace it before installing solar panels to avoid the cost and inconvenience of removing and reinstalling the panels later.

  • How much energy can a solar panel system produce?

    The amount of energy that a solar panel system can produce depends on the size and capacity of the system, the location and orientation of the panels, and the amount of sunlight in the area.
